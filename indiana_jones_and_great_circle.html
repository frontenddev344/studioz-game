Indiana Jones and the Great Circle – A Modern Adventure Worth Taking

Indiana Jones and the Great Circle brings the legendary archaeologist back for a globe-spanning adventure filled with ancient mysteries, dangerous enemies, and forgotten secrets.

Set between the events of Raiders of the Lost Ark and The Last Crusade, the game follows Indy as he investigates a series of mysterious sites connected by a hidden pattern known as the Great Circle.

Combining cinematic storytelling, exploration, puzzle-solving, and action, the game captures everything fans love about the iconic franchise while introducing a fresh modern experience.

One bad gamble can wipe out the entire team's progress...

A True Indiana Jones Adventure

Rather than focusing purely on combat, The Great Circle places exploration and discovery at the heart of the experience.

Players travel across stunning locations, uncover ancient artifacts, decipher clues, and solve environmental puzzles inspired by classic archaeological adventures.

Every location feels handcrafted, encouraging curiosity and rewarding players who take the time to explore every corner.

Exploration, Puzzles, and Action

The Great Circle balances exploration with cinematic action sequences, stealth encounters, and clever puzzle design.

Whether you're infiltrating enemy camps, navigating ancient tombs, or uncovering hidden chambers, the game constantly introduces new challenges and surprises.

Key Features:

  • Rich story-driven adventure
  • Large environments to explore
  • Creative environmental puzzles
  • Cinematic action and stealth gameplay
  • Authentic Indiana Jones atmosphere

Developer Information

Indiana Jones and the Great Circle was developed by MachineGames, the studio known for its acclaimed Wolfenstein series, in collaboration with Lucasfilm Games.

Release Date & Platform

Indiana Jones and the Great Circle is available on PC and Xbox Series X|S, delivering a cinematic adventure experience for modern platforms.
