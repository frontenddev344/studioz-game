Little Nightmares II Walkthrough

A complete Little Nightmares II walkthrough covering every major puzzle, escape sequence, and collectible location from the Wilderness to the Signal Tower.

Surviving the Pale City

Follow Mono and Six through the terrifying world of Little Nightmares II. This guide focuses on puzzle solutions, stealth strategies, and collectible locations to help you complete the game with minimal frustration.

Walkthrough Chapters

  • 🌲 The Wilderness
  • 🏫 The School
  • 🏥 The Hospital
  • 🌆 The Pale City
  • 📡 The Signal Tower

🌲 The Wilderness

The opening chapter introduces Mono and the Hunter. Expect environmental traps, stealth encounters, and your first collectibles. The Wilderness contains multiple Glitching Remains and Hats hidden off the main path.

1. Escaping the Hunter

Watch for bear traps hidden in leaves and use sticks to safely trigger them. When crossing open fields, move only when the Hunter isn't aiming his shotgun.

2. Solving the Attic Puzzle

After rescuing Six, retrieve the key from the attic and unlock the basement door. Work together to move obstacles and reach otherwise inaccessible ledges.

Collectible Tip

Explore collapsed bridges, caves, and hidden side paths carefully. Several Glitching Remains are concealed in optional areas throughout the chapter.

🏫 The School

The School is filled with hostile Bullies and the terrifying Teacher. Precision platforming and stealth become increasingly important as you progress. The chapter also contains multiple Hats and six Glitching Remains.

1. Defeating the Bullies

Use the hammer to eliminate Bullies quickly. Wait for them to charge before striking to avoid being overwhelmed by groups.

2. Library Puzzle Solution

Move carefully across suspended shelves and bookshelves. Avoid making noise near the Teacher and use timing rather than speed to reach the exit safely.

Teacher Encounter Tip

Stay hidden whenever possible. The Teacher's elongated neck allows her to search areas from surprising angles, making patience essential.

🏥 The Hospital

The Hospital introduces darkness-based puzzles, Living Hands, and the Doctor. Your flashlight becomes one of the most important tools in the game. The chapter also includes four Glitching Remains and two collectible Hats.

1. Escaping the Living Hands

Keep your flashlight aimed directly at the hands. Any moment spent looking away allows them to rush toward Mono.

2. Mannequin Puzzle

Rotate mannequins using the flashlight and move carefully through dark rooms. The puzzle relies on controlling visibility and creating safe paths forward.

Boss Tip: The Doctor

Use stealth while moving through the ceiling area. After reaching the incinerator, lure the Doctor inside before activating the furnace.

🌆 The Pale City

The city expands the game's television mechanics and introduces the mysterious Thin Man. Rooftop navigation, signal puzzles, and hidden collectibles become the focus here. The chapter contains two Hats and four Glitching Remains.

1. TV Signal Puzzle

Adjust television frequencies until the distorted hallway straightens. Follow the correct signal direction to progress deeper into the city.

2. Rooftop Traversal

Use hanging objects, cables, and moving platforms to cross dangerous gaps. Timing jumps is more important than rushing through encounters.

Collectible Tip

Check sewers, apartments, and hidden ventilation shafts carefully. Several Glitching Remains are located in optional rooms far from the main route.

📡 The Signal Tower

The final chapter transforms reality itself. Corridors twist and reshape as Mono approaches the source of the transmission and uncovers the truth behind the Pale City.

1. Corridor Distortion Puzzle

Listen for the musical signal and move through doors that align with the correct melody. Wrong choices reset the room layout.

2. Monster Six Chase

Use the music box as your guide. Strike it repeatedly with the hammer whenever an opening appears while avoiding collapsing structures.

Ending Tip

Collecting every Glitching Remain throughout the adventure unlocks the game's secret ending sequence.
