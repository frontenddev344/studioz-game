Broken Free

Escape a world of absurd rules, eccentric characters, and hilarious situations in this witty satirical adventure that pokes fun at modern society and everyday life.

Broken Free Team
Satirical Adventure Review
Updated on January 16, 2025

Broken Free follows an ordinary citizen trapped inside a bizarre system where every aspect of life is controlled by ridiculous regulations and nonsensical bureaucracy. Determined to reclaim their freedom, players embark on a humorous journey filled with quirky characters, unexpected challenges, and clever social commentary. Along the way, you'll solve puzzles, expose absurd institutions, and make choices that influence how the story unfolds in this entertaining satirical adventure.
