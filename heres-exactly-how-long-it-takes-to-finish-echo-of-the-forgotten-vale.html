Here's Exactly How Long It Takes to Finish Echo of the Forgotten Vale

If you're planning your next adventure in Echo of the Forgotten Vale, one of the biggest questions is how much time you'll need to see the story through. Whether you're rushing through the main campaign or exploring every hidden corner of the valley, your total playtime can vary significantly depending on your approach.

Main Story Completion Time

Players focused primarily on the main objectives can expect to complete Echo of the Forgotten Vale in roughly 12 to 15 hours. The campaign follows a structured narrative with several major regions, story-driven quests, and cinematic encounters that steadily build toward the final chapter.

Exploration Adds Several Extra Hours

The Forgotten Vale is packed with optional content including hidden caves, collectible relics, side quests, and environmental puzzles. Players who enjoy exploring every area and uncovering the game's lore can easily spend an additional 8 to 12 hours beyond the main storyline.

Completionist Playthrough

For those aiming to unlock every achievement, discover all secrets, and complete every side activity, a full completionist run can take between 25 and 30 hours. Tracking down rare collectibles and optional boss encounters represents the biggest time investment.

Average Playtime Breakdown

  • Main story: 12–15 hours
  • Main story + side content: 20–25 hours
  • Completionist run: 25–30 hours
  • Achievement hunting: 3–5 additional hours
  • Replay value increases with alternate choices

What Impacts Your Playtime?

Your total playtime depends heavily on exploration habits, puzzle-solving speed, combat difficulty settings, and how much optional content you choose to pursue. Players who carefully read lore entries and search for hidden treasures will naturally spend much longer in the Forgotten Vale than those focused solely on reaching the ending.

Bottom line: Most players will finish Echo of the Forgotten Vale in around 12 to 15 hours, while completionists can expect closer to 30 hours. The game's rich world and optional activities make it easy to spend far more time exploring than the main story alone requires.
