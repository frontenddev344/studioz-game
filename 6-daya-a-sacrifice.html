Facility Mystery Walkthrough

Author: Gaming Content Team | System: PC | Updated: 2026 | Version: 1.0

Introduction

This is a narrative-driven adventure featuring time-displaced scenarios and facility-based exploration. The story is set in a future research complex where the protagonist awakens with fragmented memories. Throughout the experience, the player must uncover the truth about the facility's purpose, interact with other survivors, and navigate through environmental obstacles across six days. The game emphasizes puzzle-solving, dialogue choices, and environmental interaction to achieve progression and uncover the overarching mystery.

Walkthrough

The game begins within the research facility. Retrieve the information packet located on the primary work surface.

DAY 1

The protagonist awakens in the research laboratory. Engage in conversation with the facility security officer and respond with an expression of disorientation. When the officer approaches, interact with them to obtain a written document. Deliver this document to the assistant character. Initiate dialogue with the assistant regarding the control interface. Indicate the access point in the northern section. Following the scene transition, proceed through the right doorway to access the corridor. Travel eastward to observe a secured barrier. Return westward through the corridor. Access the first door on the left side to encounter the facility director. Receive equipment from this interaction. Travel rightward and deliver the equipment to the assistant character. Conclude the day by discussing all available topics with present characters.

DAY 2

Exit through the right doorway and observe the presented scene. Access the left doorway to return to the corridor. Travel rightward and interact with the security system. After several moments, the facility controller will authorize passage. Return to the left doorway and access the corridor. Move leftward so the security officer begins advancing toward you, then return to the laboratory. After the scene concludes, utilize the communication device and contact the assistant to report that the security obstacle has been cleared. Access the right doorway to reach the corridor. Move leftward to encounter and speak with the assistant. Following the subsequent scene, access the left doorway to the corridor. Enter the first door from the left to reach the habitation chamber. Interact with the storage unit and input ACCESS, then confirm. Open the storage unit to retrieve the access credential. Return to the corridor via the left doorway. Open the third door from the left to enter the control chamber. Interact with the terminal and deactivate the first security level. Exit the control area. Locate and retrieve optical enhancement equipment from the floor in the corridor. Open the second door from the right to enter the surveillance chamber. Interact with the screen interface and select the alternate camera position. Review the recorded data through the interface. Apply the optical enhancement to the monitor screen to reveal the secured barrier access code. Access the left doorway. Travel eastward to the secured barrier location. Utilize the access credential in the receptacle positioned at the left side of the barrier. Interact with the numerical input panel at the right side and enter the discovered sequence. Use the communication device to contact the facility director to report that assistance is required for barrier access. Following the scene, exit through the right doorway to advance to day three.

DAY 3

Access the right doorway to enter the corridor. Open the third door from the right to locate the security officer. Initiate conversation regarding the lowest topic option four successive times. The officer will subsequently depart. Access the left doorway to the corridor. Open the second door from the right. Interact with the screen and select the alternative camera. Input the access sequence. Retrieve the data through the interface. Use the communication device to contact the facility director regarding discoveries within the secure chamber. Return to the corridor. Open the first door from the right to enter the biological chamber. Engage in conversation with the genetic researcher and inquire about memory recovery. Mention that you need to depart. Return through the left doorway to the corridor. Access the second door from the left. Speak with the facility administrator regarding the facility founder's identity. Discuss theories regarding the facility's research operations. Express conclusions about genetic duplication processes. Return to the corridor. Open the fourth door from the left. Attempt to interact with the facility director position; the credential is not available. Exit through the right doorway to the corridor. Travel right toward the secured barrier location. Engage the facility controller in dialogue regarding credential availability. Travel eastward through the corridor. Open the first door on the right to return to the biological chamber. Transfer the credential to the genetic researcher. Following the scene, move leftward to the corridor. Open the third door from the left to access the command chamber. Retrieve documentation resting upon the information storage unit. Exit to the corridor. Open the second door from the right to reach the surveillance area. Interact with the electrical distribution system to open it. Examine the paperwork in your inventory to discover a connecting fastener. Utilize the fastener on the electrical distribution system. Exit through the left doorway. Travel eastward to the secured barrier. Following the scene, provide the facility controller with the statement "The founder seeks the controller" to conclude day three.

DAY 4

Access the left doorway to the corridor. Open the second door from the left to discover the administrator is absent. Return to the corridor. The facility director will appear. Avoid direct contact with the facility director while traveling rightward and enter the third door from the right to access the equipment storage. Retrieve the digging implement. Exit the storage area. Move directly into contact with the facility director to transition to the habitation chamber. Access the right doorway to reach the food preparation area. Remain stationary until the facility director approaches you, then utilize the digging implement on the wall opening to complete day four.

DAY 5

Move toward the sealed enclosure and the facility controller will manifest. After the controller vanishes several moments later, a delivery mechanism will open. Utilize the delivery mechanism to retrieve a contained item. Examine the item to discover a cutting tool. Apply force to the left wall surface and an opening will emerge. Utilize the cutting tool on the entrance to facilitate escape from the enclosure. Progress downward along the descending passage to reach an interdimensional entity. Communicate with the entity and request it remain in position. Apply the cutting tool to the entity. Collect the emanating force from the entity's left section to conclude day five.

DAY 6

Open the entrance to the habitation chamber. Access the left doorway to the corridor. Travel westward to the secured barrier location. Proceed through the barrier aperture to bring the genetic duplicates into the historical mansion. Access the right doorway toward the prize collection chamber and retrieve the large blade. Access the left doorway to the main entrance. Operate the front aperture and an additional duplicate will manifest. Ascend the stairs and open the first aperture. Retrieve the thermal protection equipment. Access the subsequent aperture. Return downstairs and operate the front aperture to permit three more duplicates entry. Ascend and access the far right aperture to enter the bathing chamber. Obtain the protective garment to construct the complete outfit. Access the aperture. Descend and operate the front aperture to permit three additional duplicates entry. Access the left aperture twice to reach the food preparation space. Apply the constructed outfit on the wall opening. Following the scene, remain in position until the environment stabilizes, then apply force to the surrounding obscurity to reach the subterranean chamber. Interact with the protective covering three successive times. Proceed through the aperture. Following the scene, move rightward and interact with the dominant figure to conclude the experience.

Item Reference

Item Description
PROTECTIVE GARMENT Located in the habitation chamber in the historical mansion on day 6. Serves as one of three components required to construct the complete outfit.
CUTTING TOOL Discovered by examining the contained item within inventory on day 5. After applying force to the left wall within the enclosure, the cutting tool is utilized on the entrance barrier. Applied to the interdimensional entity on day 5.
COMMUNICATION DEVICE Available at the experience start. Utilized to contact the assistant on day 2 following clearance.

Information

This guide provides step-by-step progression through the facility mystery. Pay close attention to dialogue cues and environment interactions to successfully navigate the six-day cycle.
