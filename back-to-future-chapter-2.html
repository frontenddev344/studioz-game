Back to the Future: The Game - Episode 1:It's About Time

Author: Gaming Content Team | System: PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Mobile | Updated: 2026 | Episode: 1

Introduction

This episode begins the time-travel adventure of Marty McFly as he discovers that Doc Brown has mysteriously disappeared. In :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}, Episode 1 sets up a chain of events involving unstable timelines, missing inventions, and a journey into the past. Players are tasked with uncovering where Doc has gone and why the DeLorean has returned without him.

Prologue

The story opens shortly after a failed time travel experiment. Marty learns that Doc is gone, and his workshop is being cleared out due to financial trouble. Explore the garage and surrounding area, interact with key objects, and gather early clues about Doc’s disappearance. This section introduces basic controls, interaction mechanics, and inventory use.

Chapter 1 - Estate Sale Disturbance

At Doc’s estate sale, tension rises as familiar faces inspect his belongings. Players must search through items carefully while avoiding distractions and interruptions. Important evidence is hidden among personal inventions and documents, which will later help reconstruct Doc’s intentions.

Chapter 2 - The Time Jump

The DeLorean unexpectedly reappears with no driver, signaling that something has gone wrong in the timeline. By investigating the vehicle and analyzing recorded messages, Marty discovers that Doc is trapped in the past. The mission becomes clear: locate the correct time period and follow him.

Chapter 3 - 1931 Investigation

The search leads to the early 1930s, where Hill Valley is much more dangerous and unfamiliar. Marty must gather information from locals, examine historical clues, and piece together Doc’s situation. Some characters may help, while others create obstacles that require careful dialogue choices.

Chapter 4 - Breaking the Timeline

As more information is uncovered, it becomes clear that the timeline itself is unstable. Actions in the past are affecting the future in unexpected ways, requiring players to rethink decisions and revisit key locations. Puzzle-solving focuses on logic, item combinations, and environmental observation.

Chapter 5 - Rescue and Escape

In the final phase, Marty works to free Doc and restore balance to the timeline. Players must complete final puzzles, coordinate actions across characters, and ensure the escape route is stable. Once resolved, the DeLorean becomes the key to returning to the correct time period.
