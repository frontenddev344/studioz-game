Hollow Knight Walkthrough

A comprehensive guide to conquering Hallownest, from the Forgotten Crossroads to the final challenge.

Entering Hallownest

Welcome to the depths of Hallownest. This walkthrough focuses on efficient progression, prioritizing key ability unlocks and boss strategies to help you navigate the labyrinthine world effectively.

Walkthrough Chapters

  • ⚔️ Forgotten Crossroads & Greenpath
  • 🍄 Fungal Wastes & Mantis Village
  • 🏙️ City of Tears & Crystal Peak
  • 💧 Ancient Basin & The Abyss
  • 👑 Kingdom’s Edge & The Final Ascent

⚔️ Forgotten Crossroads & Greenpath

This is where your journey begins. The Crossroads act as the central hub of Hallownest, while Greenpath introduces the platforming and mobility challenges that define the game.

1. Clearing the Crossroads

Focus on activating the Stag Station early. Use your basic nail strikes to navigate through the infected husks. Ignore the harder-to-reach ledges for now; you lack the mobility to reach them.

2. Acquiring the Mothwing Cloak

Upon entering Greenpath, follow the path until you encounter the Hornet. After defeating her, proceed left to locate the Mothwing Cloak. Crucial: This dash ability is your key to unlocking the rest of the game.

Boss Tip: Hornet (Protector)

Stay grounded. Her dash attack is telegraphed; jump over it and strike once. Do not over-commit to combos, as she recovers quickly.

🍄 Fungal Wastes & Mantis Village

The Wastes are a test of your platforming reflexes. Use the bouncy mushrooms to traverse gaps and prepare for the intense combat of the Mantis Village.

1. Mastering Mushroom Bouncing

Practice striking down on mushrooms to launch yourself higher. This is required to cross wide pits and reach platforms that seem out of reach.

2. Acquiring the Mantis Claw

Deep within the Mantis Village, you'll find the Mantis Claw. This is the second most important movement upgrade, allowing you to cling to walls and jump off them repeatedly.

Boss Tip: Mantis Lords

Patience is rewarded. They have predictable attack patterns; avoid their downward strikes and strike back during their lunge animations.

🏙️ City of Tears & Crystal Peak

The City serves as your primary hub for upgrading your nail, while Crystal Peak introduces dangerous environmental hazards.

1. Nail Upgrades

Visit the Nailsmith in the City of Tears to upgrade your weapon. Higher damage is essential for the tankier enemies you'll face in later zones.

2. The Crystal Heart

Locate the Crystal Heart in the peak. This unlocks the Super Dash, enabling horizontal traversal over massive distances and hazardous pits.

💧 Ancient Basin & The Abyss

A somber, high-difficulty area where you will finally unlock the ultimate traversal mechanics.

1. Monarch Wings

Defeat the Broken Vessel to gain the Monarch Wings. This grants you the double-jump, allowing you to reach the most obscure secrets in Hallownest.

2. Descending into the Abyss

With the wings, you can now enter the Abyss. Here, you will uncover deep lore and the Shade Cloak, which lets you dash through enemy attacks safely.

👑 Kingdom’s Edge & The Final Ascent

The endgame. Prepare yourself for the toughest combat encounters as you head toward the Black Egg.

1. The King's Brand

Battle Hornet again in Kingdom's Edge to receive the King's Brand, which grants you entry to the deepest reaches of the Kingdom.

2. The Hollow Knight

Return to the Forgotten Crossroads and enter the Black Egg. Ensure your Charms are optimized—focusing on *Quick Slash* and *Unbreakable Strength*—before the final confrontation.
