Introduction

:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1} is a psychological horror adventure set inside a shifting residential building where reality constantly bends. Players must navigate unstable corridors, unpredictable floor layouts, and mysterious entities while trying to uncover what is happening inside the structure. This walkthrough covers exploration, puzzle-solving, survival strategies, and progression through each unstable level of the building.

Prologue

The game begins inside a seemingly normal apartment hallway. Start by exploring nearby doors, interacting with objects, and learning basic movement controls. Early signs of distortion appear subtly—flickering lights, shifting shadows, and inconsistent room layouts. The prologue introduces how the building reacts to player movement and sets up the core mechanic of unpredictable floor transitions.

Chapter 1 - The First Floor Shift

After the initial exploration, the building begins to change unexpectedly. Hallways no longer lead where they should, and doors may open into altered versions of familiar rooms. Carefully observe environmental changes, as subtle visual differences often signal safe paths. Avoid rushing through corridors, as incorrect choices can trigger dangerous encounters or resets.

Chapter 2 - Strange Neighbors

You begin encountering unusual residents who may or may not be trustworthy. Some provide cryptic hints, while others behave unpredictably or disappear when approached. Pay attention to dialogue patterns and environmental cues. Certain interactions unlock hidden routes or reveal shortcuts between unstable sections of the building.

Chapter 3 - Breaking Reality

Reality distortion intensifies, introducing looping corridors and overlapping rooms. Puzzle elements become more complex, requiring players to track inconsistencies in architecture and sound design. Use observation carefully—objects that shift positions or reappear may indicate alternate paths or hidden triggers necessary for progression.

Chapter 4 - Hidden Levels

Deeper exploration reveals concealed floors that do not exist on the building’s map. These levels often contain environmental puzzles tied to memory, timing, or spatial awareness. Expect unpredictable transitions between spaces. Backtracking may lead to entirely new configurations, so always reassess surroundings before proceeding.

Chapter 5 - Escape Route

In the final phase, the goal shifts toward escaping the unstable structure. Players must piece together clues gathered throughout earlier sections to locate a stable exit point. Final puzzles require combining environmental logic with previously discovered patterns. Precision and patience are key, as incorrect decisions may loop you back into earlier sections.

Survival Tips

Tip Description
Observe Carefully Small visual changes often indicate major environmental shifts.
Don’t Rush Moving too quickly can trigger dangerous or unstable transitions.
Remember Layouts Memorizing corridors helps identify when reality changes.
Test Interactions Some objects behave differently depending on the current floor state.
Trust Patterns Repeated anomalies often hint at safe progression paths.
