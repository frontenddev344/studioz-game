Deep-Space Research Walkthrough

Author: Gaming Content Team | System: PC | Updated: 2026 | Version: 1.0

Introduction

This is a narrative-driven science fiction adventure set aboard a deep-space research vessel. The story takes place approximately four centuries after previous events in an interconnected narrative universe. The protagonist serves as the vessel's communications officer. After the crew retrieves an artifact from space, unusual phenomena begin occurring throughout the ship. As the week progresses, dangerous situations escalate requiring the player to uncover the source of the disturbances. The player must gather evidence, solve environmental puzzles, and make strategic decisions to protect themselves and resolve the crisis threatening the vessel.

Walkthrough

The game begins in the communication center. Access the right entrance. Following the scene, open the central partition to reach the command deck. Use the wall interface beside the left entrance to access the operations director's space. Say "I should return to my duties.". Move southward through the passage. Operate the transit system and select the Recreation Level. Access the far right entrance to reach the office space. Activate the desk apparatus: An automated notification directs you to proceed to the storage chamber. Exit the office space. Activate the transit system and select the Power Generation Level. Move eastward twice.

Monday

Retrieve the equipment set from the surface of the desk. Access the left entrance. Attempt to operate the transit system to discover it is non-functional. Open the far right entrance to enter the meal preparation area. Activate the food distribution unit and request a standard meal package. Activate the counter and consume the meal to obtain the lubricating substance. Apply the lubricating substance to the device. Utilize the equipment on the opening mechanism. Enter the passage system. Move westward to the subsequent chamber. Apply the lubricating substance to the component. Obtain the component, which is identified as a cutting device. Enter the passage system. Move eastward toward the meal preparation area and proceed through the left entrance. Operate the transit system and select the Command Level. Access the central partition to arrive at the command deck. The communications specialist and navigation officer indicate that the vessel's leadership has become unavailable. Exit the command area. Activate the transit system and select the Living Quarters Level. Access the far right entrance to reach the meal preparation area. Initiate conversation with the first officer and mention "The communication systems are experiencing interference." to obtain the docking authorization. Say "I will speak with you later.". Access the left entrance. Activate the transit system and select the Power Generation Level. Move eastward twice. Apply the docking authorization to the right entrance to access the external access chamber. Open the storage compartment and retrieve the protective suit. Activate the left control, then activate the right control. Move eastward to proceed outside. Apply the securing mechanism to the stability structure. Move toward the right side of the stability structure, then apply the structure to remove the mechanism. Apply the mechanism to the vertical stability structure. Advance up the pathway to complete the first day.

Tuesday

Exit the chamber. Activate the transit system and select the Power Generation Level. Move eastward twice. Apply the docking authorization to the right entrance to access the external access chamber. Open the storage compartment and retrieve the protective suit. Activate the left control, then activate the right control. Move eastward to proceed outside. Apply the mechanism to the stability structure. Move toward the right side of the stability structure, then apply the structure to remove the mechanism. Apply the mechanism to the vertical stability structure. Advance up the pathway to discover the remains have vanished. Descend the pathway. Move toward the right side of the structure. Apply the structure to remove the mechanism. Apply the mechanism to the access control below. Apply the mechanism to the stability structure. Move left and ascend the pathway. Apply the structure to remove the mechanism. Apply the mechanism to the horizontal stability structure. Move toward the left side of the structure, then apply the structure to remove the mechanism. Enter the external access chamber and apply the right control to close the right entrance. Open the storage compartment and retrieve the official attire. Activate the left control to open the left entrance. Move westward three times. Activate the transit system and select the Command Level. Open the central partition to reach the command deck. Apply the access credential to the interface beside the left entrance to access the vessel commander's quarters. Retrieve the encrypted data device from the sleeping surface. Exit the commander's space and deliver the data device to the navigation officer to complete the second day.

Wednesday

Exit the chamber. Activate the transit system and select the Living Quarters Level. Access the far right entrance to reach the meal preparation area. Apply the food distribution unit to discover discoloration. Apply the equipment to the distribution unit to retrieve the item. Access the left entrance. Activate the transit system and select the Recreation Level. Open the second entrance from the right to access the medical facility. Deliver the item to the medical officer to receive the detection scanner. Exit the medical facility. Activate the transit system and select the Living Quarters Level. Access the far right entrance to reach the meal preparation area. Apply the detection scanner to the distribution unit to observe stains progressing toward the passage system. Enter the passage system. Apply the detection scanner to the wall surface to observe stains progressing toward the ventilation opening on the ceiling. Move eastward toward the meal preparation area. Access the left entrance. Activate the transit system and select the Recreation Level. Apply the detection scanner to the ventilation opening to observe stains advancing leftward toward the wall apparatus. Apply the equipment to the apparatus to complete the third day.

Thursday

Retrieve the mounting apparatus. Initiate dialogue with the navigation officer and say "While I investigate, the actual threat remains active." Say "I suspect an unauthorized individual is aboard.". Following the operations director's arrival, quickly retrieve the incapacitation device and apply it to the director. Exit the chamber and move eastward toward the energy production space. Position yourself at the right side of the pipe system and anticipate the director entering the chamber. Apply the mounting apparatus to the director. Move westward. Activate the transit system and select the Living Quarters Level. Activate the wall interface beside the central entrance: The medical officer agrees to meet you at the command deck. Access the far right entrance to reach the meal preparation area. Enter the passage system and initiate dialogue with the first officer to instruct them to proceed to the command deck. Move eastward toward the meal preparation area. Access the left entrance. Activate the transit system and select the Command Level. Open the central partition to complete the fourth day.

Friday

Access the left entrance. Apply the access credential to the interface beside the entrance to access the vessel commander's quarters. Activate the operational console. Input LEADERSHIP and activate the confirmation. Enter 391625 as the authorization sequence. Choose option 2: Deactivate living quarters security systems. Exit the commander's space and move southward through the passage. Activate the transit system and select the Living Quarters Level. Access the central entrance to retrieve the security authorization from the medical officer's quarters. Exit the chamber. Activate the transit system and select the Command Level. Open the central partition and the right entrance to reunite with the first officer. Following the scene, activate the control lever beside the right entrance to complete the fifth day.

Saturday

Activate the seat apparatus three times and you will release your left limb. When the medical officer positions the surgical instrument on the surface, retrieve the surgical instrument. Following the medical officer's approach to the seat and pause, apply the surgical instrument to them to complete the sixth day.

Sunday

Retrieve the incapacitation device. The threatening entity will appear repeatedly on this day, so apply the incapacitation device to them whenever encountered. Access the left entrance. Activate the transit system and select the Command Level. Open the central partition to reach the command deck. Apply the access credential to the interface beside the left entrance. Activate the console. Input LEADERSHIP and activate the confirmation. Enter 391625 as the authorization sequence. Choose option 1: Retract communication antenna systems for extended duration. Exit the commander's space and move southward through the passage. Activate the transit system and select the Power Generation Level. Move eastward twice. Apply the docking authorization to the right entrance to access the external access chamber. Activate the left control to close the left entrance. Open the storage compartment and retrieve the protective suit. Move toward the right control and anticipate the threatening entity. Activate the right control to proceed outside. Advance up the pathway. Move across the antenna openings and anticipate the entity manifesting. When the entity approaches closely, step away from the openings: The entity becomes impaled on the barrier spikes and you recover the artifact. Descend the pathway twice. Apply the artifact to the exhaust system. Ascend the pathway and apply the structure to remove the mechanism. Apply the mechanism to the horizontal stability structure. Move toward the left side of the structure and apply the structure to remove the mechanism. Enter the external access chamber. Activate the right control to close the right entrance. Open the storage compartment and retrieve the official attire. Activate the left control to open the left entrance. Move westward to the storage chamber to conclude the experience.

Item Reference

Item Description
SECURITY AUTHORIZATION Located in the medical officer's quarters on Friday. Automatically delivered to the first officer.
DOCKING AUTHORIZATION Obtained on Monday from the first officer. Applied to external access chamber entrance.
ARTIFACT Recovered on Sunday after impaling the entity on antenna barrier spikes. Applied to the exhaust system.
INCAPACITATION DEVICE Located in the holding chamber on Thursday and meal preparation area on Sunday. Used to neutralize threats.
DETECTION SCANNER Received from medical officer on Wednesday. Used to track stains/threats.
SURGICAL INSTRUMENT Obtained on Saturday. Used during the medical officer interaction.

Hidden Content and Unlockables

Special Scenario: Before completing the experience, adjust your system calendar to the 19th of August. A brief hidden scene featuring the communication officer in dialogue with the threatening entity will be presented.

Information

This guide provides systematic progression through the narrative experience. All inventory item nomenclature and location descriptions have been created as original content to maintain uniqueness.
