Agatha Christie - Hercule Poirot: The First Cases Walkthrough

Author: Gaming Content Team | System: PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch | Updated: 2026 | Version: 1.0

Introduction

Agatha Christie - Hercule Poirot: The First Cases follows the legendary detective during one of his earliest investigations. Set at the luxurious Enderstone Manor, Poirot must uncover the truth behind a shocking incident that occurs during a family celebration. This guide provides a complete walkthrough covering clue collection, suspect interrogations, mind map deductions, puzzle solutions, and the final resolution of the mystery.

Prologue

Begin by exploring the reception area of Enderstone Manor. Speak with every guest available and familiarize yourself with the investigation interface. Examine interactive objects highlighted throughout the environment and collect all introductory clues before advancing the story. The prologue serves as a tutorial introducing evidence gathering, observation mechanics, and Poirot's deduction system.

Chapter 1 - The Gathering

After the incident interrupts the celebration, inspect the crime scene thoroughly. Search for physical evidence, interview witnesses, and note any inconsistencies in their statements. Use the mind map system whenever new evidence becomes available to establish logical connections. Be sure to investigate every room unlocked during this chapter before proceeding.

Chapter 2 - Investigating the Guests

Focus on interviewing each member of the Van den Bosch family and their guests. Compare testimonies against collected evidence and revisit locations whenever new clues reveal additional interactions. Several optional observations become available during this chapter and provide valuable context for later deductions.

Chapter 3 - Hidden Motives

New evidence uncovers long-hidden family secrets. Continue building deduction chains within the mind map and investigate restricted areas of the manor. Pay close attention to personal relationships, financial disputes, and conflicting alibis as these form the foundation of the case.

Chapter 4 - Connecting the Clues

By this stage most evidence should be collected. Reconstruct the timeline of events using all available witness accounts and physical clues. Solve remaining environmental puzzles and complete every major deduction sequence to unlock the final confrontation.

Chapter 5 - Solving the Case

Gather the suspects and present your conclusions. Complete the final deduction sequences carefully, selecting evidence that directly supports each accusation. Once all critical connections are established, Poirot reveals the truth behind the mystery and identifies the responsible party.

Investigation Tips

Tip Description
Inspect Everything Examine every highlighted object to avoid missing important clues.
Review Evidence Often New clues frequently unlock additional deduction opportunities.
Question Everyone Exhaust all dialogue options with each suspect whenever possible.
Use the Mind Map Regularly complete deduction chains to unlock new investigation paths.
Revisit Locations Previously explored rooms may contain new evidence later in the story.
