Guap Preview – A Chaotic Co-op Roguelike Survival Game

What happens when chaotic co-op survival gameplay meets roguelike mini-game mechanics? That's exactly the idea behind Guap, a fast-paced multiplayer roguelike where players must fight their way out of an increasingly dangerous facility using in-game challenges.

Instead of exploring abandoned planets or collecting scrap, you and your teammates are thrown into a high-risk environment where every mini-game outcome could either save the group or ruin the entire run.

Designed around tension, teamwork, and unpredictable outcomes, Guap transforms its in-game challenges into survival mechanics. Each in-game day comes with a score target your team must reach before time runs out.

One bad decision can wipe out the entire team's progress, making every move feel meaningful and dangerous.

A Co-op Survival Roguelike Experience

At its core, Guap is a multiplayer roguelike built for up to four players. The game focuses heavily on risk management, communication, and adapting to random events.

Unlike traditional roguelikes, Guap adds intense survival pressure to every decision. Players aren't simply chasing high scores—they're trying to stay alive long enough to escape the facility.

The atmosphere combines neon-lit arcade visuals with dark, unsettling environments that create a constant sense of tension.

Dynamic Gameplay and Daily Challenges

Every in-game day introduces randomized mechanics and game rotations. This keeps matches unpredictable and prevents players from relying on a single strategy.

Some rounds may focus heavily on card-based mini-games, while others push players toward high-risk challenge mechanics with larger score rewards.

Players Must Carefully Decide:

  • When to cash out
  • Which teammate takes the risk
  • How much money to pool together
  • Whether to play safely or go all-in

Mini-Games Available in Guap

Blackjack

Score higher than the target without going over the limit.

Roulette

Pick numbers, colours, or combinations to match the outcome.

Mines

One mistake instantly ends the round.

Plinko

Drop chips through multiplier boards.

Limbo

Set your multiplier and time your exit at the right moment.

Sic-Bo

Dice rolls with varied outcomes across different risk levels.

Rugged

Collect your score before the counter resets.

Unbox

Huge rewards with extremely low odds.

The Golden Dome

Defense-inspired strategy gameplay with multiple outcomes.

F-Racer

Choose your frog and race to the finish line.

Visual Style and Atmosphere

Guap leans heavily into a dark neon arcade aesthetic mixed with psychological tension. Flashing lights, distorted audio, crowded rooms, and sudden environmental changes create a stressful but exciting atmosphere.

The first-person presentation makes every decision feel personal, especially during high-stakes moments when teams are risking everything to survive another round.

Developer Information

Guap is being developed by indie studio BoMassy, with a strong focus on replayability, multiplayer chaos, and unpredictable roguelike systems.

Release Date & Platform

Guap is currently planned for release on March 6, 2026 and will launch on PC via Steam.
