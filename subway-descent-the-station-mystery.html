Subway Descent: The Station Mystery

Introduction

"Subway Descent" is a point-and-click horror adventure following Thomas, a commuter trapped in a nightmarish, distorted station, and Officer Chen, who investigates the dark secrets behind his disappearance. This guide details all objectives, puzzles, and choices.

Mechanics & Controls

Controls: Interact/Move (LMB), Undo/Exit (RMB), Run (Double-click LMB), Highlight Interactive Points (Space key).
Navigation: Use the Interaction Menu (Mouth for dialogue, Eye for examine, Hand for collect) to navigate the station's distorted layers.

Walkthrough

Prologue: Nightfall

After the interrogation, explore the station corridor. Examine the broken departure boards and ticket machines. Interact with Victoria in the booth and collect the STATION MAP. Retrieve the MAINTENANCE KEY from the emergency box and attempt to use it on the service door before calling Marcus.

Chapter 1: The Platform

Search for the EMERGENCY FLARE and TRANSIT PASS. Use the pass to unlock barriers. Complete the timing-based action sequence in the tunnel. Inside the distorted station, collect the RADIO TRANSMITTER and solve the gate code puzzle using nearby documentation.

Chapter 2: Level Descent

Investigate the train cars to collect PASSENGER IDENTIFICATION, the EMPLOYEE BADGE, and the BRIEFCASE. Acquire the ELECTRICAL SCHEMATIC and PROTECTIVE GLOVES to safely access the control room and retrieve the MASTER KEY-CARD.

Chapter 3: Chen's Investigation

As Officer Chen, gather INVESTIGATION FILES and INCIDENT REPORTS. Interview staff to retrieve SHIFT LOGS and MAINTENANCE RECORDS. Uncover RESEARCH DOCUMENTS in the supervisor's office to reveal the station's ancient, pre-modern foundations.

Chapter 4: Deeper Exploration

Navigate the deepest, ancient levels using the key-card. Complete timing challenges to evade crystalline entities. Collect RITUALISTIC OBJECTS and the SEAL-BINDING ARTIFACT. Decipher the terminal sequence to stabilize the protective seals.

Chapter 5: Convergence

Thomas and Chen unite. Restore the three seals using the sequence: 1) Binding Artifact + Protection Stone, 2) Transit Mechanism + Foundation Object, 3) Historical Record + Containment Crystal. Survive the final entity reaction to restore the station's normal appearance.

Chapter 6: Emergence

Decide whether to report the events or maintain secrecy. Exit the station to find the city seemingly unchanged. Both characters are permanently affected by their experiences.

Epilogue: Months Later

Officer Chen discovers new incident reports matching previous patterns. The cycle begins anew, and she returns to the station to investigate personally as Thomas observes from a distance.

Information

Achievement Tip: Aim for 200+ deductive observations throughout your playthrough to unlock the "Master Observer" achievement.
