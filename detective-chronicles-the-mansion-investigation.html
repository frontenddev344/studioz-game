Subway Descent: The Station Mystery

Introduction

Thomas, a commuter, finds his subway station transformed into a nightmarish labyrinth. Simultaneously, Officer Chen investigates a series of strange disappearances at the same location. This guide covers every objective, puzzle, and narrative choice.

Mechanics & Controls

Controls: Interact/Move (LMB), Undo/Exit (RMB), Run (Double-click LMB), Highlight (Spacebar).

Walkthrough

Prologue: Nightfall

Examine the malfunctioning ticket machines, schedule board, and static-filled emergency phone. Speak with Victoria; collect the STATION MAP and MAINTENANCE KEY from the emergency box. Attempt to use the key, then call Marcus.

Chapter 1: The Platform

Collect the EMERGENCY FLARE and TRANSIT PASS from the lockers. Use the pass to bypass barriers. Succeed in the timing-based tunnel action sequence. Collect the RADIO TRANSMITTER and solve the gate code using documentation found on the nearby shelf.

Chapter 2: Level Descent

Investigate the train cars to collect PASSENGER IDENTIFICATION, the EMPLOYEE BADGE, and the BRIEFCASE. Acquire the ELECTRICAL SCHEMATIC and PROTECTIVE GLOVES to safely enter the control room, then retrieve the MASTER KEY-CARD.

Chapter 3: Chen's Investigation

Switch to Officer Chen. Gather INVESTIGATION FILES and INCIDENT REPORTS. Interview staff to obtain SHIFT LOGS and MAINTENANCE RECORDS. Find the hidden RESEARCH DOCUMENTS in the supervisor's office to uncover the station's ancient history.

Chapter 4: Deeper Exploration

Navigate the deepest, ancient levels. Evade crystalline entities via timing challenges. Collect RITUALISTIC OBJECTS and the SEAL-BINDING ARTIFACT. Solve the terminal sequence to stabilize the seals.

Chapter 5: Convergence

Thomas and Chen unite. Restore the three seals using the sequence: 1) Binding Artifact + Protection Stone, 2) Transit Mechanism + Foundation Object, 3) Historical Record + Containment Crystal. Survive the final entity reaction to normalize the station.

Chapter 6: Emergence

Decide whether to report the events or maintain secrecy. Exit the station to find the city normal, though the protagonists have been permanently changed.

Epilogue: Months Later

Officer Chen notices the cycle of disturbances beginning anew. She decides to return to the station, while Thomas observes the news from afar, setting up an ambiguous conclusion.

Information

Achievement Tip: Aim for 200+ deductive observations across all chapters to unlock the "Master Observer" achievement.
