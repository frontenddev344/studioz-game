Adventure Mystery Walkthrough

Author: Gaming Content Team | System: PC | Updated: 2026 | Version: 1.0

Introduction

This is a narrative-driven adventure game featuring mystery-solving mechanics and environmental puzzles. The story follows a protagonist who arrives at an old estate and discovers themselves caught in mysterious circumstances alongside other characters. Throughout the experience, players must uncover secrets, collect evidence, and piece together what has occurred within the manor walls. The game emphasizes exploration, dialogue choices, and logical problem-solving to progress through five distinct story chapters.

Chapter 1

Begin in the study room on the upper level. Investigate the window by interacting with it to learn it's sealed. Exit through the doorway. In the hallway, you'll observe another character moving away. Proceed downstairs to the main entrance. Access the right passage three times to meet a character who mentions gathering everyone for discussion. Enter the left passage three times, then climb the stairs. Move to the right section of the hallway and speak with a female character about the upcoming meeting. Return downstairs and go through the left passage three times to reach the exterior. Examine the tree near the house to hear unusual sounds. Speak with the tree and a character will descend. Return indoors. Enter the right passage three times to access the main gathering room. Engage all characters in conversation about various topics to advance to Chapter 2.

Chapter 2

Exit through the left passage four times to reach outside. Head toward the garden area and interact with a male character. Discuss his current activity and his search intentions. Mention a specific location that provides clues. Continue the conversation peacefully. Return to the house. Navigate right twice, ascend the stairs, and enter the nearest room to reach the library. Take a colored book from the third shelf on the left bookcase. This item provides valuable information. Return to the garden and give this information to the character. Receive a searching tool in exchange. Move westward and use the tool on the water piping system. Trace the pipeline path to discover a mound. Investigate the mound to reveal a hidden compartment. Attempt to open the compartment. Continue west to observe the pool area has been emptied. Descend the ladder into the empty pool to conclude Chapter 2.

Chapter 3

Exit through the left passage four times: A transportation vehicle now appears outside. Enter the house, navigate right twice, and go upstairs. Enter the nearest room to access the library. Speak with a character and inquire about their intentions. Ask if they would assist your investigation. They agree to follow you. Exit the house and approach the vehicle to retrieve tools. Use these tools on the storage shed door. Open the shed and collect two specific items inside. Exit and wait for your companion to arrive. Use one item on a large outdoor plant, and your companion will help remove it. Return indoors. Navigate right twice to reach the main entrance. Collect a printed document. Go upstairs and use tools on the second door from the right. Open the cabinet inside and retrieve a bound journal. Access the window and climb outside. Use an item on a loose architectural element to traverse to another window opening. Enter through that window. Collect an item from the furniture. Open the drawer and retrieve another journal. Exit through the doorway. Enter the library near the top of the stairs. Retrieve different colored books from various shelves. In your inventory, read through the collected documents in sequence. You'll realize you need to locate a specific location. Retrieve a darker colored book. Read this book completely. Exit the library, go downstairs, and navigate right twice to reach the viewing room. Collect a cord from this space. Navigate left five times to exit the house. Locate a small branch. Return to the food preparation area and open the middle storage section to reveal a mineral substance. In your inventory, combine the cord with a specific soft item. Combine this new item with the branch. Apply this combination to the mineral. Navigate right twice, climb stairs, and enter the third door from the right. Use your created combination on the sleeping surface. Your creation now provides directional guidance. Return to the food preparation area to complete your next action. Examine your combined item in your inventory to receive guidance. Follow these directions until you reach a specific location below. Use a tool on an obstruction to reveal hidden remains. A loud noise occurs upstairs afterward. Climb the stairs and navigate right five times to see a large object has shifted. Collect this object to complete Chapter 3.

Chapter 4

You awaken in the storage structure. Communicate through the window to hear a female character outside. Speak again to continue the dialogue. Address her accusations with your perspective. State that an external force controlled your actions. Confirm that this force influenced you previously. Acknowledge your emotional state about past events. She provides an item by sliding it under the door. Collect this item to find a small metal tool. Use this tool on the door mechanism. Open the door to exit the storage shed. Enter the house, navigate right twice, ascend the stairs, and open the door on the right side of the hallway to access the washing room. Shortly after a female character enters, a figure with specialized equipment appears. Wait until this character stands on a particular floor covering. Use this action to conclude Chapter 4.

Chapter 5

Respond to the female character's inquiries with any answer. Enter the room behind your current position and speak with a male character to receive three items. State you'll depart soon. Exit the room, go downstairs, and navigate right to reach the resting area. Collect sharp implements from a piece of furniture. Navigate left three times to access the food preparation area. Combine the sharp item with protective clothing to create a strip. Combine a protective mask with this strip. Combine the mask-strip combination with a large blade. Use this creation on the mineral substance. Proceed through the hole to the lower level and use your creation on chain restraints. Return upstairs. Navigate right twice, ascend the stairs, and enter the far right door to access the washing room. Examine your creation to receive specific directions. Follow these directions to locate the correct position. Combine the sharp item with your creation. Use the blade on wall surfaces to reveal skeletal remains. Collect these remains. Exit the washing room. Access the library near the top of the stairs. Collect one of the darker books from the right shelving unit. Exit the library. Go downstairs and navigate right three times. Read through the darker book. Use the remains on a specific spot in front of a fire structure. Apply protective clothing on these remains. Navigate left twice to reach the sitting room. Communicate with the female character and request she meet you in a specific location. Navigate left, climb the stairs, and enter the third door from the right. Speak with the male character and request he proceed to the location you mentioned. Return to the first location you referenced. Collect a weapon from above the fire and transfer it to the female character. Transfer a soft item to the male character. Use the darker book on yourself to conclude the game.

Item Reference

Item Description
PROTECTIVE CLOTHING Obtained through conversation with a character in Chapter 5. Combined with sharp implements to create a strip. Later applied to discovered remains.
INFORMATION DOCUMENT Obtained through conversation at the conclusion of Chapter 1. Required reading before accessing advanced materials in the library.
COMBINATION ITEM (BRANCH + CORD) Created by combining cord with soft item, then adding branch. Used on mineral substance to create a directional guide.
ADVANCED TEXT Acquired by collecting a darker colored book after specific conditions are met. Must be read before using remains in Chapter 5.
REFERENCE MATERIAL Obtained by collecting lighter colored books from library shelves. Must be read before accessing advanced texts.
CUTTING IMPLEMENT Located in the resting area. Combined with protective clothing to create a strip. Later separated from other items and used on architectural surfaces.
CORD Located in the viewing room. Combined with soft item to begin item combination chain.
CLIMBING TOOL Available from the start. Used on architectural features to enable traversal.
ACCESS TOOL Obtained from a vehicle exterior. Used on locked doors and barriers.
SMALL TOOL Obtained through window dialogue in Chapter 4. Used on door mechanisms.
MINERAL SUBSTANCE Located in food preparation storage. Used in combination with created items to activate special properties.
SEARCHING IMPLEMENT Received from a character in Chapter 2. Used on infrastructure systems.
SOFT ITEM Located on sleeping furniture. Combined with cord to begin combination chain. Later transferred to a character.
BRANCH Located exterior of house. Combined with created items.
PROTECTIVE MASK Obtained through character conversation in Chapter 5. Combined with strip to progress combination sequence.
LARGE BLADE Obtained through character conversation in Chapter 5. Combined with mask-strip combination. Later retrieved and used on surfaces.
MASK-BLADE COMBINATION Created by combining mask-strip with large blade. Used on mineral substance in Chapter 5.
SKELETAL REMAINS Found by using blade on bathroom surfaces. Used on fire location in Chapter 5.
WEAPON Located above fire structure. Transferred to female character in final scene.
